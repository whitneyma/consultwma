Register a Business in Trinidad & Tobago

If you own or operate a business in Trinidad and Tobago, you must register with the Companies Registry and obtain a certificate of registration or incorporation if it is a company. Sole Proprietor

A sole trader, or sole proprietorship, is a type of business structure where one individual owns and manages the affairs of the business. As a sole trader, you are the business and are responsible for all aspects of the business, including profits and debt.

Requirements:

  • Two forms of ID (National ID, DP, Passport)
  • A clear headshot holding one of the IDs
  • Working email address for account login
  • Residential and postal address (if different)
  • Electronic birth certificate with pin number
  • Supporting document for name changes (e.g., marriage certificate/deed poll)

Non-profit Organization

Non-profit organizations are legal entities managed by two (2) or more controllers/directors, that aim to benefit the public or society, rather than generate profit for its owners. Their focus is on creating positive change in communities by addressing social, cultural, environmental, or humanitarian issues. An NGO (Non-Governmental Organization) is an NPO that operates independently from any government.

Requirements:

  • Two forms of ID (National ID, DP, Passport)
  • A clear headshot holding one of the IDs
  • Working email address for account login
  • Residential and postal address (if different)
  • Electronic birth certificate with pin number
  • Supporting document for name changes (e.g., marriage certificate/deed poll)
