Hi! I'm Whitney.

I am an independent business consultant and registered teacher from Trinidad and Tobago. I have qualifications in Psychology and Human Resource Management from the University of the West Indies, and my mission is to help businesses enhance their processes and empower learners to achieve their goals.

As a mother of one, I understand the need for balance and aim to create efficient solutions to streamline daily tasks. I am a problem-solver who thrives on innovation, and I am motivated to continually learn and share my knowledge with others. My interest in human behaviour helps me to improve my practices and methods, and I am able to create effective strategies tailored to individual needs.

I am a believer in Christ, and through faith, I am working towards building a community to advocate for lifelong learning. I am also dedicated to supporting mental wellness and fostering growth in business and education. I look forward to collaborating with you!
