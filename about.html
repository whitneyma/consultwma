About Me

I believe that success is rooted in meaningful connections and purposeful actions. Professionally, I am a graduate of the University of the West Indies (BSc. Psychology; minor Human Resource Management) with 12+ years of experience in banking, finance, and business administration. I am also a registered teacher in Trinidad & Tobago, and I combine my passion for education with my professional skills. My mission is to provide tailored business solutions, expert guidance, and learning support to entrepreneurs, students, and communities across Trinidad & Tobago, and beyond!


Outside of these endeavours, I am a proud mother, cat lover, and a passionate advocate for mental health and education. My story is one of resilience, faith in God, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge, and I draw upon these experiences to inspire and empower my clients and community.
